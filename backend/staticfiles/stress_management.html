Stress Management Tips

Here are some techniques that can help you manage stress:

  • 🧘‍♂️ Practice mindfulness meditation daily
  • 🏃‍♀️ Engage in regular physical activity
  • 💤 Ensure adequate and quality sleep
  • 🌿 Take short breaks throughout the day
  • 💬 Talk to a friend or family member about your feelings
  • 🎶 Listen to calming music or sounds
